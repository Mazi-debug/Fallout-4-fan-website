What Is Fallout 4?

Fallout 4 is an open-world RPG where players take on the role of the Sole Survivor, a person who emerges from an underground vault known as Vault 111 after being cryogenically frozen before the nuclear war. The game blends exploration, combat, dialogue choices, and role-playing elements. Player decisions can influence the story and determine the fate of the Commonwealth.

The Setting: The Commonwealth

The Commonwealth is a dangerous and unstable region filled with:

  • Abandoned cities and collapsed infrastructure
  • Mutated wildlife and hostile raiders
  • Small settlements trying to rebuild society

Despite the destruction, traces of old-world technology, culture, and secrets remain scattered across the land.
