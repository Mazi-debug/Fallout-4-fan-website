LORE & WORLD VIEW

A World After Nuclear War

In the Fallout universe, nuclear war broke out in the year 2077, destroying most of human civilization in a matter of hours. The survivors either hid in underground vaults or struggled to live on the surface. Two hundred years later, the world has not fully recovered. Technology exists alongside extreme poverty, and morality is shaped by survival rather than law. Vaults and Experiments Vaults were built by the Vault-Tec Corporation and were advertised as safe shelters. However, many vaults were secretly designed to run social and scientific experiments on their inhabitants. Vault 111, where the player begins, was intended to test long-term cryogenic freezing on humans.
